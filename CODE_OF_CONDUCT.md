# Code of conduct

We want participation in Expo Turbo to be safe, respectful, and technically constructive.

Contributors must:

- engage with people and ideas respectfully;
- welcome corrections and different levels of experience;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, and publication of private information;
- keep technical disagreement focused on observable behavior and evidence.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when conduct is harmful to the project or its community. Report conduct concerns privately to a repository owner rather than opening a public issue containing personal information.
